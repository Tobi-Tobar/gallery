EYE SEE YOU (2023)

Featured in a group exhibition called ART OFFICIAL, hosted at the Hammer Theater in San Jose, CA. This piece is a 3D enviroment, a cave of eyeballs met with the viewer to expirience the fact that many are expecting much of you and are always watching. No matter where you go, you'll always be watched.
